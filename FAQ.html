FAQ

Why The Good Part?
The Good Part recognizes all humans as part of one large family.
Everything that happens to the part is useful for the whole therefore by allowing users to contribute to causes across the globe at no expense to themselves we can improve the world as well as our own lives.


What percentage of funds will be donated?
Always 100% of funds will be donated.


Will crypto be exchanged for Fiat?
As 100% of funds will be donated there is never a need for cryptocurrency to be transferred into fiat. Binance funds will be shifted into a single cryptocurrency for donation purposes.


When will donations be made?
Donations will be sent initially when $15USD has been raised. Upon reaching sufficient volume donations will be made on the 21st of each month.


How are charities selected?
All non-profits worldwide are eligible to receive funds provided they accept Cryptocurrency as a form of payment.

Can I suggest a charity?
You can E-mail support@thegoodpart.world or contact @TheGoodPartOrg to add a charity to the donation list.


I've signed up to an exchange but the homepage counter hasn't increased?
It isn't currently possible to retrieve affiliate numbers via API so this is completed manually by me. Due to this there may be some delay between users registering and the homepage updating. Currently only Binance and FTX users are included in the count.
